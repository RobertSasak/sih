Ad Venous Engorgement

Engorgement of the venous sinuses was evaluated on post-gadolinium T1-weighted MPR images based on the shape of the outer contour of the superior sagittal sinus (SSS) and of the dominant transverse sinus.

Under normal circumstances, the inferior border of the middle portion of the transverse sinus and lateral border of the SSS usually exhibit a concave or straight border, which usually becomes convex in case of intracranial hypotension.
