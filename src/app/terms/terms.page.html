This tool is meant to be used only by medical professionals to predict the likelihood of a spinal Cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) leak in patients with Spontaneous intracranial hypotension (SIH). Its clinical usefulness to influence medical decisions has not been tested.

The full manuscript has been published in JAMA Neurology in February 2019.
doi: 10.1001/jamaneurol.2018.4921
PMID: 30776059
